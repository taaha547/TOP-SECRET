Tables are made through the table tag. Then there is this tag called tr (stands for table row). Inside you can put either th (Table heading) or td (Table data).
another interesting tag is the colspan and rowspan which allows you to merge cells.
Notice that it allows you to merge both horizontally and vertically.
Apart from that we also see that the table data isn't erased but shifted accordingly.
